Who We Are

Ben and David started Blue Canoe back in the spring of 2000. Previous to that David, a Twin Cities native, was involved in music, growing a family and also restoring hardwood floors on the weekends starting as far back as 1992. Ben, grew up in South Dakota, was a musician, artist and family man with 3 boys and wife. Ben and David met each other through music and played together in a few different musical projects. Seeing an opportunity to start a company in a growth service field that would be gratifying to their artistic sides they started Blue Canoe. Our goal is to be different than other companies by providing great service and outstanding quality at a competitive price.

What We Do

The core of our business is refinishing hardwood floors in residential homes, but thats just one of the things offered. Blue Canoe has extensive experience in the installation of new hardwood floors, both unfinished and pre-finished. Interlace patching to marry new flooring to old. Custom staining. Offering low V.O.C. finishes as well as two component epoxy finish not to mention tried and true oil modified polyurethane. Doing all work to minimize the dust in your home by employing new low dust sanding techniques.

How We Do It

Blue Canoe uses the best available materials to insure the best possible results. Using higher quality finishes means better flow, better sheen, greater durability and overall look. All wood is purchased from high quality hardwood flooring suppliers who are trusted with the care of hardwood and regulation of humidity to insure the wood installed in your home is more climate stable. Blue Canoe's equipment is newer and able to contain more of the dust, preventing massive clean-up at the end of your projects. Blue Canoe responds immediately to customer phone calls, and has a firm belief in fast customer follow up.
